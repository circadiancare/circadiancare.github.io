About us

Circadian Health is a group of healthcare providers involved in providing essential primary care services for our patients through Teleconsultation, offline clinics and home-visits. We, as doctors, understand the unnecessary hassles patients go through from traveling exceedingly far distances and long waiting hours at the OPD for minor health issues. Our doctors are trained general physicians and specialists in various medical disciplines practicing in different hospitals, offering teleconsultation services for patients at the comfort of their home.

Be it for preventive care for lifestyle advice, curative care for minor illness and chronic care for lifestyle disorders, contact circadian health services.
